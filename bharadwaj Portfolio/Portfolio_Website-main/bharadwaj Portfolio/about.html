About Me

I am a highly motivated and detail-oriented Master’s student in Computer Science at Rivier University, with a strong academic foundation and hands-on experience in software development, frontend engineering, and cloud computing fundamentals. I possess solid expertise in Java programming, including object-oriented design principles, data structures, and application development, along with practical experience in building modern, responsive, and user-friendly web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and contemporary frontend frameworks.

In addition to frontend and core programming skills, I have foundational knowledge of cloud computing concepts and services through Amazon Web Services (AWS), including cloud architecture basics, deployment models, and scalable infrastructure design. I am deeply interested in designing and developing scalable, efficient, and reliable software systems that solve real-world problems and enhance user experiences.

I am passionate about continuous learning, staying up-to-date with emerging technologies, and improving my technical and problem-solving abilities. I actively work on personal and academic projects to strengthen my full-stack development and cloud engineering skills, with a focus on writing clean, maintainable code and following industry best practices. My long-term goal is to become a highly skilled Software Developer and Cloud Engineer, contributing to innovative, high-impact projects, collaborating with diverse teams, and delivering scalable and efficient technical solutions that drive business success.
